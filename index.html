HOW DOES SMOKING AFFECT YOUR BODY?

Cigarettes kill half of all smokers in America.

Over 480,000 people die every year in the United States from illnesses associated with tobacco use. This means that 1 in every 5 deaths are caused by smoking cigarettes.
